Key Insights for Board Consideration

Unmatched Diversity Leadership

TFA-WA delivers 56% teachers of color—66% higher than traditional programs and 47% higher than other alternative routes. This positions TFA-WA as the state's most effective pipeline for diversifying the teaching workforce.

Holistic Diversity Approach

Beyond racial diversity, TFA-WA brings socioeconomic diversity with 48% first-generation college students and 56% from low-income backgrounds, ensuring teachers who deeply understand student challenges.

Proven Academic Impact

Research shows TFA corps members increase student achievement by approximately 23 additional days of learning per year, with alumni improving at double the rate of non-TFA teachers over their first five years.

Scalable Solution for Urgent Need

With 88% of Washington students aspiring to postsecondary education but significant achievement gaps for students of color, TFA-WA offers a proven, scalable model to address both teacher shortages and diversity needs simultaneously.
